Chair Yoga Sequence

Gentle Yoga Sequence

This gentle yoga sequence takes place entirely on your back, and if you don't have them, you don't need a yoga mat or props to do it. Enjoy this sequence before bed or any time you just want to stretch out and relax.

7-minute Yoga Sequence

This gentle 7 minute yoga session does not require any previous experience with yoga or props of any kind, no yoga mat or stretchy pants needed, either!
